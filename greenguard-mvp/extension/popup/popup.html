🎯 What is Greenwashing?

Greenwashing is the practice of making misleading claims about environmental benefits to appear more sustainable than they actually are.

🚨 Common Greenwashing Tactics

  • Vague Language: "Eco-friendly" without specific details
  • Misleading Labels: "Natural" doesn't mean environmentally safe
  • Hidden Trade-offs: Highlighting one green attribute while ignoring others
  • False Claims: Unsubstantiated environmental benefits
  • Irrelevant Claims: Promoting absence of harmful substances that are already banned

✅ How to Spot Authentic Sustainability

  • Look for specific, measurable commitments
  • Check for third-party certifications
  • Research the company's overall practices
  • Verify claims with independent sources
  • Be skeptical of perfect environmental claims
